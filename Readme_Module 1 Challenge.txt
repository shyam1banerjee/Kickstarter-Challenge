# Kickstarting with Excel

## Overview of Project
Purpose of the project is to help Louise plan the launch of her play "Fever" based on data for past plays 


### Purpose
To Help Louise in planning the launch of her play
To help her set a funding goal for her play

## Analysis and Challenges
The past plays are analysed based on the launch date and the funding goal to determine the optimum level of funding.
To understand the most appropriate time of the year to launch the play

### Analysis of Outcomes Based on Launch Date
The analysis of the Launcg date graph shows that the best time to launch the play is the month of May.
That she should avoid the months of December and January

### Analysis of Outcomes Based on Goals

Successful Plays had goals less than $5000 or between $30000 to 45000.

### Challenges and Difficulties Encountered
For meeting the goal of 30000 to $45000 she need more pledges and the launch date should also be maintained around May

## Results

- Conclusion based on Launched Date
Avoid launching the play in the month of December and January
may and June Launch has a better chance of Success
